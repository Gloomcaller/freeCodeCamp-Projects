Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

This rich and creamy chocolate fudge is a classic dessert that's easy to make and perfect for holidays or special occasions. Made with simple ingredients, it melts in your mouth with every bite.

Best served at room temperature, this fudge can be stored for weeksâ€”if it lasts that long!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Line an 8-inch square baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt chocolate chips, condensed milk, and butter over low heat, stirring constantly.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract and salt.
  4. If using nuts, fold them into the mixture.
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepared dish and smooth the top.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until firm.
  7. Cut into squares and serve.
