Baklava Recipe

A sweet, rich pastry made of layers of filo dough filled with chopped nuts and sweetened with syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 package (16 oz) phyllo dough
  • 1 1/2 cups unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 cups finely chopped walnuts or pistachios
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Mix chopped nuts with cinnamon in a bowl.
  3. Unroll phyllo dough and cover with damp cloth to prevent drying.
  4. Place 2 sheets of phyllo in prepared dish, brush generously with butter.
  5. Repeat 4 times to create 8 layered sheets.
  6. Sprinkle 2-3 tablespoons of nut mixture evenly over phyllo.
  7. Add 2 more phyllo sheets, butter, then nuts, repeating until all nuts are used.
  8. Top with remaining 6-8 phyllo sheets, buttering each layer.
  9. Cut into diamond or square shapes before baking.
  10. Bake for 50 minutes until golden and crisp.
  11. While baking, make syrup by boiling sugar, water, honey and vanilla for 20 minutes.
  12. Pour hot syrup over baked baklava and let cool completely before serving.
