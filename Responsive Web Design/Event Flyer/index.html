International Color Day Celebration

Come and join the International Color Day event and celebrate colors, palettes, designs and all things that make our world more colorful!

  • When: March 21
  • Where: Old Town Square
  • Entry: Free!
  • Start: 10:00

Event Highlights

  1. Grand opening parade featuring giant floating color sculptures at 11:00
  2. Interactive color mixing workshops for all ages at 12:00
  3. Color theory lectures by renowned designers at 14:00
  4. Live mural painting with audience participation at 17:00
  5. Spectacular fireworks display synchronized to a color symphony at 21:00

Special Guests

  • Leatrice Eiseman - Executive Director of the Pantone Color Institute
  • David Carson - Graphic design legend and color innovator
  • Emily Oberman - Award-winning designer and color strategist
  • The Chromatics - World-famous color performance artists
  • Local school children presenting their color-inspired artwork

Food & Drinks

  • Rainbow-themed food trucks offering colorful culinary creations
  • DIY smoothie station where you can mix your own colorful drinks
  • Color-changing lemonade (changes hue as you stir!)
  • Edible paint cookies for creative dessert decorating
  • Vibrant vegetarian and vegan options showcasing natural food colors
  • Signature "Spectrum Cocktails" for adult attendees
