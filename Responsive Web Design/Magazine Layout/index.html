Chill Game Review

Our Top Picks for May 2025

Whether you prefer a farm management simulation or a creative city builder game, we have you covered with two unanimously beloved picks for this month’s recommended chill games. Dive into a world of homesteading within the charming Stardew Valley, or let your imagination run wild with Townscaper.

Stardew Valley

Are you tired of the constant hustle and bustle of the city? Do you wish you could move to the countryside and live off the land? Then Stardew Valley is the game for you! Grow your own crops, forage for food in the lush nature, or join the Adventurer’s Guild in fighting monsters. Stardew Valley gives you the freedom to explore the world at your own pace, while simultaneously helping the community and building relationships with the villagers. With unlimited gameplay and customization, the game is certain to provide hours of fun and relaxation. Stardew Valley is available for PC, consoles and mobile devices, making sure you always have access to your favorite farm life simulation game.

Townscaper

Imagine a world where architectural wonders rise at your fingertips. Where serene fishing villages transform into intricately designed palaces. A world where the only limit is your creativity. That world is contained within Townscaper, a small, yet powerful game that makes your ideas come to life. Its simplicity of use and distinct visual style enable you to create elaborate coastal towns in a matter of seconds. With creativity as its main objective, Townscaper is ideal for people wanting a calm yet engrossing experience. This experimental town builder game is available on multiple platforms, allowing you to experience it on any device.
